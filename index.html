About Ringette

Ringette is a uniquely Canadian winter sport that's as challenging as it is enjoyable.
It was created by Sam Jacks in 1963 and has since been dubbed the fastest sport on ice.

The main objective of ringette is to use a stick to hit a ring into the opposing team's goal. The team with the highest score at the end wins.
